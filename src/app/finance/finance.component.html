Finance

The contribution of the members are the only source of income of the Kudumbayogam. The following funds are organized and operated. Financial Management is jointly carried out by the President, General Secretary and the Treasurer.

  1. Kshemanidhi (Welfare Fund)
    2.  The Welfare Fund was formed and started in 1976 to help those members who are financially backward, and to give them medical as well as educational aid. The said works are carried out from the interest of the money collected through endowments, donations etc.
  2. Sthiram Fund (Static Fund)
    3.  The Static fund started in 1988 is that amount of money deposited in the Bank. The expenses for general activities of the Kudumbayogam and the expenses for Annual meeting are partly met out of the interest accrued from the Static Fund.
  3. Educational Scholarship
    4.  This programme is started in 2002 with the intention of encouraging financially backward intelligent students in education upto 12th standard, who have passed from 4th standard and who are selected from the specially conducted test.
  4. Charithra Fund (History Fund)
    5.  This is to raise the Fund for publishing the Kudumbayogam History and the Website.
  5. Vivaha Sahaya Nidhi (Marriage Aid Fund)
    6.  Marriage aid fund has been raised to give financial assistance for the marriage of the women of those families who are economically backward.
  6. Bhavana Nirmana Sahaya Nidhi (House Construction Aid Fund )
    7.  This fund is aimed at, to assist for constructing and maintaining residential houses, for those who are not financially well.
